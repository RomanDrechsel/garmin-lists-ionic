General Data Processing Information

In this privacy policy, we inform you about the type, scope, and purpose of the processing of personal data within our mobile app.

Types of Processed Data

  • Usage data: Data on the use of the app (e.g., functions accessed)
  • Location data: Data on the location of the device if the location function is used

Purpose of Data Processing

  • Operation of the app: To provide the basic functions of the app
  • Advertisement: For the display of personalized advertising by Google AdMob

Legal Basis for Data Processing

Your data is processed on the following legal bases:

  • Consent (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR): If you have consented to the use of your location data
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR): For the processing of usage data to ensure the functionality and security of the app and to display advertising

Google AdMob

This app uses Google AdMob, a service for integrating advertising from Google Inc. (“Google”). Google AdMob uses so-called “cookies”, text files that are stored on your device and that enable your use of the app to be analyzed.
You can find further information in Google's privacy policy.

Location Data

If you have consented to the use of location data, we use this exclusively to improve the user experience and to provide location-based functions. This data is not transmitted to third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right:

  • To request information: You can request confirmation at any time as to whether personal data concerning you is being processed (Art. 15 GDPR).
  • To request correction: You have the right to rectification and/or completion of your personal data (Art. 16 GDPR).
  • To request deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data stored by us (Art. 17 GDPR).
  • To request the restriction of processing: Under certain conditions, you can request the restriction of the processing of your personal data (Art. 18 GDPR).
  • To object to the processing: You can object to the processing of your personal data (Art. 21 GDPR).

Security

We use technical and organizational security measures to protect your data managed by us against accidental or intentional manipulation, loss, destruction, or access by unauthorized persons.
